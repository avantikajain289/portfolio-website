Catharsis

Click Image to Zoom

Overview

This project was designed as a native mobile app for both Android and iOS platforms utilizing iOS and Material design guidelines. A movie app that curates movies for a week based on various genres. The app adds seven movies each week within all genres. This gives the user a more curated list within the genres they like and from directors around the globe. There is an additional function for the artistically leaning user’s that find inspiration from cinematic color-scapes and moods. Artists and designers can create beautiful color palettes utilizing this unique function using an image, time of the movie, and URL’s.

Problem

Existing media platforms have too much content and no one platform has the range all user’s enjoy. It invariably takes more time to find something to consume then to consume the content. People inevitably purchase multiple subscriptions to consume content and media that they enjoy on each platform. This does not create a cohesive and consistent algorithm or suggested choices for user’s. There is a market for user’s that enjoy quality over quantity. Film and cinema’s artistic expression is enjoyed by most but there aren’t a lot of platforms that cater to these needs. User’s would like to explore cinema from around the world, given the exposure and opportunity.

Competitive Analysis

For Media

The competitive analysis for media channels like Mubi, Criterion Collection, and Netflix brought about insights about users needs, pain points, and preferences for consuming content. These were some of the insights collected from the analysis.

For Colour Palettes

The competitive analysis for color palette apps like Coolers (iOS) and Pigments (Android) were useful in understanding the overall flows and functions to incorporate into the app. There were utilitarian functionalities along with a visually stunning layout and UX.

User Pain Points

Understanding user pain points from the analysis and overall UX process lead to interesting and insightful revelations about the feelings, thoughts, actions, and behaviors of user’s around media and content consumption.

Defining the Problem

Who?

The app is for users that are art and cinema lovers. Users that want quality over quantity would be the target audience.

What?

Tasks to accomplish:

  • Select and play movies.
  • Create color palettes using movie stills and save them.
  • Add a movie to a watch list.

When?

The audience will use this app at home, on-the-go, work or any time of the day.

Where?

They will use this app on their mobile screens. It can be accessed anywhere the user would like at home, work, during their commute (not while driving).

Why?

The app caters to the user’s entertainment needs and admiration for art. The app can utilize various features of the operating systems of both iOS and Material design for a better experience.

Persona's

Diving deeper into the process and to understand the user along with the situations within which they would need access to the app assists in building and figuring out pain points and tasks needed to fulfil their unique needs and desires.

User Flow Diagram

User flow’s are a great way to analyse and specify tasks that need to be completed along with their steps. It assists in the app’s flow and structure, as a framework, to plan and execute the next steps of the process.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

Low fidelity wireframes are built upon the user flow diagram and tasks created earlier. This helped structure and differentiate between the two different platforms’ (iOS and Android) wireframes and components.

Overall, these wireframes facilitate in the creation of a cohesive brand with a style guide that can be applied to both while keeping the guidelines for Human Interface and Material design.

Final Design

This user-centric process, with its restrictions and guidelines taken from Human Interface Guidelines and Material design, nurtured the creation of Catharsis.

The final design consists of a lean UX interface, with haptic feedback considerations, and guideline-specific components that provide solutions to the pain points and needs mentioned earlier by user’s.
