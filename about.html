My Story

I am a New Delhi-based graphic designer turned UI/UX designer focusing on visual systems, user experience, and interface design. I apply my knowledge of digital and print media to present meaningful solutions to a problem by creating connections with the users. Coming from a country with a rich history and traveling/living in new places has given me the opportunity to explore and interact with diverse cultures. This has impacted my skills in a way that helps me design being culturally conscious and user-centric. I enjoy exploring various fields within design and art. My time in New Delhi, San Francisco, and Toronto has aided my curiosity and explorations of new ideas within multi-media design, explorative audio-visual design, dance, crafts and collaging, volunteering, and technology.
